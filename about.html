About CanDo

“An all-in-one web app that encourage career explorations through resources and community”

CanDo was created as a submission for UC Berkeley’s Hack Month 2020 hackathon. The web app was created by 4 individuals from different schools and backgrounds but all shared similar challenges with career exploration and resource accessibility.

CanDo was conceptualized to combat these challenges and advocate for those wanting to make a change in their career or education path. Wheter you’re an emerging college student, transfer student, wanting to make a career change, or someone who wants to learn a new skill, CanDo will connect you with the community and resources for your personal growth and advancement.
